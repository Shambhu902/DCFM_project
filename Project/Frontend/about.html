About DCFM

The Differentiated Case Flow Management (DCFM) System is a specialized judicial platform designed to streamline case management processes for judges and court staff. Our system provides intelligent tools to prioritize, track, and manage cases efficiently.

Our Mission

To enhance judicial efficiency and reduce case backlogs through innovative technology solutions that support timely justice delivery while maintaining the highest standards of legal process.
